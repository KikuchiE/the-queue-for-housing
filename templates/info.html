Housing Queue Management System

Our system simplifies and automates the process of applying for government or municipal housing. With intelligent priority calculations, real-time availability checks, and seamless integration with government registries, it ensures a transparent and efficient housing distribution process.

Why Choose Our System?

  • Easy Application Process – Submit your housing request with detailed living conditions in just a few steps.
  • Fair and Automated Queue System – Prioritization is based on objective criteria like income, family size, and special needs.
  • Real-Time Updates – Track your application status and receive timely notifications about progress in the queue.
  • Full Control for Administrators – Manage applications, issue document update reminders, and ensure smooth operations through a dedicated admin panel.
  • Seamless Integration – Connect with government registries for accurate and up-to-date information.
